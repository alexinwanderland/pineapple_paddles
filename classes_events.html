yoga

Experience yoga on the water this summer and ride the waves of self discovery. Take a break, enjoy nature, relax, and add a playful thrill to your yoga practice. The amazing skyline view and a blissful savasana are like no other. Classes are done entirely on the boards on Lake Michigan and all levels are welcome! Lessons are recommended for first time paddlers but our skilled teachers will guide you no matter where you are in your journey. We can’t wait to share this beautiful practice with you!

*All prices are in USD. Cash and credit cards are accepted!

fitness

Start out as a warrior and end in a Zen state with this workout. If you are looking for an amazing outdoor workout that will benefit any paddler and bring your mind to a zen state, then come try Urban Kai’s Warrior Zen Workout. The workout is done partly on land and partly on the water using stand-up paddle boards, trx bands, stability balls, bands and kettlebells. Come out and get your mind right and get fit at the same time!

*All prices are in USD. Cash and credit cards are accepted!

social paddles

Our Community Paddles give you the opportunity to paddle with other members of the local community with the goal of growing the Chicago SUP community as a whole! These paddles are geared toward a wide range of ages and skill levels and include discounted rates and unique offerings in a fun group atmosphere!

*All prices are in USD. Cash and credit cards are accepted!
