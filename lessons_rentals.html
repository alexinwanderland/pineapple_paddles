Want to do your own thing, or learn how to? Our classes are the perfect way to learn ideal paddleboarding techniques and leave comfortable on a board. Already feeling fresh? Grab a rental and go rock out! Check out our offerings below...

paddle 101

An introductory 1-hour group lesson to stand up paddle boarding which includes the teaching of basic skills and a paddle around Harbor Island. A great way to get started in the world of SUP! We offer 1 or 2 SUP 101 classes per week and it’s a great way to meet other SUP enthusiasts. Please RSVP if you need a board so we know about how many to bring out.

$30

paddle 102

Take your skills to the next level. The next step after you have completed SUP 101. In SUP 102 you will learn more advanced techniques such as advanced turning methods and race and safety techniques all in the same beautiful setting as SUP 101. You can even take both classes in the same day! Please RSVP if you need a board so we know about how many to bring out.

$30
